Results

Each submission was reviewed by every member of the jury and received an initial score. All submission were then discussed and reevaluated in a discussion with all jury members which lead to a final score and selection of winners. The authors of the submissions were invitied to present their work at the IEEE VIS conference 2025 in Vienna. Afterwards, the winning entries were announced.
