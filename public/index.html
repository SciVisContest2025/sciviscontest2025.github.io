2025 IEEE SciVis Contest

Material Science Challenge: discovery of novel materials


Welcome to the 2025 IEEE SciVis Contest website. This contest is held annually as part of the IEEE VIS Conference.
In 2025, the conference and this contest will be held in Vienna, Austria, and will focus on the visualization-based discovery of
novel materials in recylcing.

On this website, you can find information on the provided data, the visualization tasks, as well as the submission process.
If there still are questions, feel free to contact the contest chairs or join the Google Group. .
