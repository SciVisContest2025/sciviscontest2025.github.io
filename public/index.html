2024 IEEE SciVis Contest

Sonar Imaging for Hydrothermal Systems

Welcome to the 2024 IEEE SciVis Contest website. This contest is held annually as part of the IEEE VIS Conference.
In 2024, the conference and this contest will be held in Tampa Bay, Florida, USA, and will focus on the visualization of
Sonar Imaging for Hydrothermal Systems.

On this website, you can find information on the provided data, the visualization tasks, as well as the submission process.
If there still are questions, feel free to contact the contest chairs or join the Google Group. .

The deadline has been extended to August 7th, 2024.
